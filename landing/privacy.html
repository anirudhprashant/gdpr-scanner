Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

GDPR Scanner collects minimal data necessary to provide our service:

  • Scan data: URLs scanned and compliance results (stored locally in your browser)
  • Account data: Email address (Pro/Agency tiers only)
  • Payment data: Processed securely by Stripe

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide compliance scanning services
  • Generate compliance reports
  • Send service-related communications
  • Improve our product

3. Data Storage

Scan results are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. We do not store your scan results on our servers unless you explicitly export a report.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties except:

  • Payment processing (Stripe)
  • Legal requirements

5. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Erase your data
  • Restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Object to processing

6. Contact

Data Protection Officer: dpo@gdprscanner.ai

7. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page.
